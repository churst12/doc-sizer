Doc-Sizer

Font

Fonts are sorted by size. Click the sort button so switch order of fonts.

Spacing

Change the line spacing to separate each line by a certain distance.

Margins

Change the top and bottoms to alter the amount of space your text lines are allowed to take.

Punctuation Sizer

This changes the punctuation size to a larger or smaller font size than that of your text. This tool is adjustable via the settings menu.

Period Spacer

This adds two spaces after all the periods instead of just one.

Contractions

This automatically expands or contracts all avaiable contractions. Contractions can be viewed or added in the settings.
